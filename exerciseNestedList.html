Abhi's day out

Daily routine of Abhi on a vacation

  • Wake Up
  • Offer Prayers to Surya Dev
  • Freshen up
  • Prepare breakfast
  • Toast a bread:
    • put bread on a hot pan with some oil
    • let it turn tan brown
    • take it out of the pan and put aside
  • Prepare the omelette:
    • crack an egg into a bowl
    • put salt,red chilli powder and whisk it properly
      1. red chilli powder
      2. salt
    • put some oil in a pan
    • pour the mixture on the hot pan
    • let it cook on both sides for 1 min each and serve hot
  • Get ready after washing the dishes and head outside
  • Roam on the streets with friends for an hour or so
  • come home
  • sleep Cozu are tired AF!
